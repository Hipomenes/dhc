This is a static site writen in plain text, renderd by Jekyll through the kramdown processor, and hosted freely in Github Pages. The design is a fork of Dennis Tenen’s site, with minor modifications by yours truly. In its desing, structure and general feel, my site is inspired by ideas about Minimal Computing, [Just enough] design, and in general, by the conviction that all graphic design beyond typography is tacky and moraly objectionable. Ultimately, this site is dedicated to Cosma’s Home Page, aptly described as the “worst home page ever.” Cosma Rohilla Shalizi’s Home Page is so bad that it doesn’t even use a CSS file. It has no index and no other structure than that which emerges from the sum of its internal hyperlinks.1 And yet, it is a monument to the possibilities of human curiosity and the promise of hypertext.

David, Redwood City, CA, 2018.
